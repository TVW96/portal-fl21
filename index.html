About Me

For some reason, the site of children in helmets make's me giggle. I used to teach skateboarding with a non-profit organization named Y.E.S.(youth employment skateboarding). I worked with the program for a number of years and got my first project management experience from them organizing skateboarding events throughout the greater seattle. I've also taught snowboarding with a program I once attended through the YMCA as a youth, the name of this program was T.S.B.(the service board).

I'm a student at Seattle Central College studying computer science. I'm am also a current intern of the Spring 2022 cohort here at Year Up Puget Sound. Math has been one of my strong suits since I was a child, and I take pride in my critical and logical thinking skills.
